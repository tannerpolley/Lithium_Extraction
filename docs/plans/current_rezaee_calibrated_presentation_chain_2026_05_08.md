## Preferred Claim

A calibrated Rezaee Section 3.2 DOE-basis distribution surrogate generates a 523-row TDS-Li-O/A matrix for produced-water process screening and returns finite Li extraction, Na extraction, selectivity, and distribution coefficients for PrOMMiS/IDAES transfer.

## Required Caveat

The selected Smackover clean Li/Na stream is outside the original Rezaee DOE Na/Li range. The surrogate is appropriate for process screening and interface development, but high-Na/Li experimental validation remains future work.
